About

Hi! I'm Adrienne and this is my solo project. I make music that includes (but is not limited to) synthesizers, drum machines, samples, congas, drones, Python controlled MIDI, biosonification data, disco grooves, pop sensibilities, 80s nostalgia that I can never truly experience because I was born in the 90s, + more. :) I have performed in New Music Ensembles, avant-garde groups, orchestras, a jazz band, and an electronic duo.

I am available for live shows, film and video game scoring, art installations, collaborations, etc. I love to program in Python. I live in Nashville, TN. Feel free to reach out.
